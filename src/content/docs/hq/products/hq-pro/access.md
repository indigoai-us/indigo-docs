Access to HQ Cloud and HQ Pro is **demo-gated**: a short demo is the front door. Whether you're a prospect requesting access or a teammate being invited, you enter the same flow, and access opens once your demo is complete.

## The access flow

1. **Request access.** Submit your email and company or team on the request-access page. This creates a pending access request — it is not yet claimable.
2. **Book a demo.** Pick a time on the scheduling page. Your request advances to "demo booked."
3. **Get granted.** After the demo, an admin grants your access. The invite email is sent and your membership becomes claimable.
4. **Claim and onboard.** Accept the invite, then finish onboarding — install HQ, join your team, run your first sync, and start the beginner training. See [Team Onboarding](/hq/products/hq-pro/onboarding/).

A super-admin can grant access directly, without a demo, for internal or expedited cases.

## Good to know

- Until access is granted, an account cannot be claimed — the gate blocks the claim with a clear error and no membership is created.
- Existing members and invites are unaffected: they are treated as already granted, so nothing changes for current users.
- Admins can see and act on pending and booked demo requests — and grant or revoke access — from the demo queue.
- A teammate invited by an admin enters the same demo-gated flow rather than receiving an immediately-claimable invite, so everyone arrives through the same front door.
